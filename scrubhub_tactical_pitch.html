Chapter 1: The Landscape & Problem

The Churn Problem

Car wash businesses rely on predictability. But the industry faces a massive retention challenge.

Involuntary Churn (Payment Failures)

Around 3% of members drop off each month simply because their credit cards expire, payments fail, customers don't update payment info, or license plates change.

These customers didn't want to leave—the system just kicked them out. Without a way to quickly notify them, that revenue is permanently lost.

Voluntary Churn (Low Engagement)

Memberships are like gym subscriptions—if customers forget they have it, they will eventually cancel. When we don't actively remind them of their savings or engage them, the value drops.

In Q3 2025, voluntary churn was 4.7% and credit card churn was 2.9%, bringing total churn to 7.6%.

Source: Carwash.com Q3 2025 Report
Chapter 1: The Landscape & Problem

The Data & Loyalty Gap

1. Operating Blind (No Customer Data)

When customers pay at a kiosk, we rarely know who they are, how often they visit, or when they stop coming. By not capturing behavior, we are unable to build targeted Custom Audiences for ads, and we miss crucial remarketing and re-engagement opportunities.

2. No Loyalty or Reward System

Without incentives, local customers have no reason to stay loyal to one wash. Competitors who offer rewards directly steal these customers.

"The research says it’s five to six times more expensive to find a new client than it is to reactivate an old client."

Source: American Express

New Features

Cross-Platform App

A single application for customers to manage their entire relationship with ScrubHub.

ROI: Elevates brand credibility to match massive chains and keeps ScrubHub "in their pocket."

1-Tap Plan Management

Update cards, add vehicles, or change plans without a clunky web portal.

ROI: Reduces Operational Debt. Frees up on-site staff from acting as customer support.

Proactive Churn Prevention

Automated push notifications for expiring credit cards.

ROI: Plugs the 3% Leak. Attacks involuntary churn, recovering thousands of dollars in monthly revenue.

LPR Integration & Value HUD

Talks to DRB tunnel hardware to show: "You saved $38 this month!"

ROI: Prevents Voluntary Churn. Proves the financial value of the $25/mo plan in real-time.

The "We Miss You" Trigger

Automatically detects if a paying member hasn't washed in 21+ days and sends a targeted offer.

ROI: Re-engages At-Risk Members before they cancel.

Weather-Triggered Alerts

Connects to local weather APIs to push automated "Post-Rain Recovery" deals.

ROI: Protects Retail Volume exactly when the wash is normally empty.

Lightning Deals

Scarcity-based push notifications sent to opted-in users on slow days.

ROI: Drives Impulse Visits, giving staff more opportunities to upsell.

Gift-a-Wash Mechanism

Allows members to buy a wash and text a link/QR code to a friend in 15 seconds.

ROI: Viral Acquisition with zero added marketing spend.

Operations Analytics Dashboard

A backend view showing conversion rates, total churn, and attributed campaign revenue.

ROI: Total Visibility. Undeniable proof the digital system generates ROI.
